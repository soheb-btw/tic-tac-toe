How To Play

Tic Tac Toe 2.0

Objective:

Achieve Tic Tac Toe within the larger grid by winning mini Tic Tac Toe games.

Game Rules

Grid Structure:

The game consists of a 3x3 grid, where each cell contains another 3x3 mini Tic Tac Toe grid.

Initial Player:

Player 1 starts the game.

Making Moves:

Players take turns to make a move by clicking on an empty cell in the mini Tic Tac Toe grid. The first player marks "X," and the second player marks "O."

Navigation:

After each move, the next player must select a cell in the mini grid corresponding to the cell clicked by the previous player. Example: If Player 1 clicks the top-middle cell in a mini grid, Player 2 must make their move in the top-middle cell of the corresponding larger grid.

Winning a Mini Grid:

When a player achieves Tic Tac Toe within a mini grid, that mini grid is won. The corresponding cell in the larger grid is marked with the winning player's symbol ("X" or "O"). The won mini grid becomes disabled, and players cannot make moves in it.

Navigating Through Won Grids:

If a player's turn corresponds to a won mini grid or a disabled cell in the larger grid, they can make their move in any available cell of the larger grid.

Winning the Game:

To win the entire game, a player must achieve Tic Tac Toe within the larger 3x3 grid.

Game End:

The game ends when a player wins the larger grid or when there are no more valid moves left.

Tips:

Think strategically to block your opponent and plan ahead to win the larger grid. Pay attention to won mini grids to make strategic moves in the larger grid.

Have Fun Playing Tic Tac Toe 2.0!
